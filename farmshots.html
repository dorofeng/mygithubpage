I was hired as a freelancer by Farmshots, an on-campus startup, to redesign their product offering document that was to be presented to VC firms to raise revenue for their company. Farmshots uses drone and satellite imagery to inform farmers about the quality of their land.
Because this would be one of the first impressions of the company for VC representatives, it was extremely important to make this pamphlet eye-catching and easy to read. When it came to the layout of the document, I first abstracted the core values that this product offering was supposed to convey: that farmowners have a problem, and this startup can provide the best solution to this problem. With this in mind, I displayed the company product features by first profiling the problem, and then explaining the solution that the startup has for this particular problem.
