This app was created while learning Swift from the Bitfountain iOS8 with Swift Immersive online course. The framework and design elements were designed in Photoshop and then coded into a functional app using XCode and Swift. Check out the project on Github here.


The Challenge:


The goal of this game is to maintain a profit each day of the game. As soon as the user runs out of money to purchase new inventory, the game ends. The player starts with $10, 1 Lemon, and 1 Ice Cube. Each additional Lemon costs $2, and each additional Ice Cube $1. When "Start Selling" is pressed, it creates a ratio of lemonade acidity that affects sales (this is based on ratio of lemons to ice cubes). For example, 1 lemon and 3 ice cubes will produce a ratio of .333. A randomly generated number of customers visit the stand each day, and each customer has a random taste preference (between 0 and 1). These customer taste preferences are then compared to a range of lemonade acidities (customers with a preference between 0 to 0.4 favors acidic lemonade, 0.4 to 0.6 favors equal parts lemonade, 0.6 to 1 favors diluted lemonade). The lemonade ratio is also being compared to a different set of ranges: > 1 = acidic lemonade, equal to 1 = equal portioned lemonade, and < 1 = diluted lemonade. The customer will only purchase the lemonade if its acidity suits their preferences. The weather is also randomly generated and plays into the amount of customers that show up. The weather icon depics the weather, and changes each day. At the end of each day, the amount earned from lemonade sold will be updated.


The Tools:


App elements wireframed and created in Photoshop. App was built in XCode.
