Hi, I'm Dorothy Feng

Student, Designer, Developer

I am currently a junior at Duke University pursuing dual majors in Computer Science and Psychology, and an Innovation & Entrepreneurship Certificate. I'm a strong believer in a multidisciplinary approach to solving problems. Art and design have been lifelong passions of mine, but more recently I have become interested in intersecting design, psychology, and technology, namely in web/mobile development and product design.

When I'm not learning code or picking up freelance art/design gigs, I like to golf, bake pumpkin-flavored anything, and obsess over Game of Thrones.
